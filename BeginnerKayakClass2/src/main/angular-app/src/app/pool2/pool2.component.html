Tips & Reminders

  • Use nose plugs, unless you amazingly don’t get water up your nose. It also helps save air, allowing you more time so you don’t rush your roll.

  • Feel free to bring a diving mask or goggles if you are a visual learner.

  • Use the gear checklist to ensure you have all required equipment.
