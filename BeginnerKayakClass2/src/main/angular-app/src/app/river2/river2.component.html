Etiquette

  • Leave No Trace!
  • Smoking: be respectful of those around you! Take your butts home ;)
  • Driving and parking: don't park/stand in the road or private drives.
  • Private property: respect it! Violate only if rescue/life/death – never convenience!
  • Modesty and bathroom habits:
    • No flashing – don’t ruin a kid for life ;)
    • Use man-made toilets when available.
    • Bury poo and pack out TP.
  • Be respectful to other river users: anglers, rafters, other kayakers, canoeists, wildlife.

Safety

  • Safety is everyone's reponsibility.
  • Wet exit, self-rescue, swimming with gear & being towed.
  • Never stand up in the river, unless you can crawl!
  • Understand river signals.

River Features

Rivers have many features; some are good, some are bad.

During your kayaking career you will boat on rivers with many different features. Knowing what to do, which to avoid and what to get excited about is a skill that comes with time.

Try to keep your eyes open for river features and see if you can identify them while we’re on the river.
