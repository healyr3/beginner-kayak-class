River Trips

  • Find a trip or river: WKC trips or organize your own
  • No WKC trips on the calendar? Consider Paddle Trails

When to sign up

  • No later than the Wednesday before a weekend day trip
  • For overnight trips call a week or more in advance

Ask the trip leader the 4W’s!

  • When: meet time
  • Where: meet location (put-in or take-out)
  • What: classification, length, characteristics, current level, hazards of river
  • Who: group size and skills, how many vehicles

Tell the trip leader

  • Names of ALL attendees, ages (if minors)
  • Your training, experience, and/or skills

Arrange yourself

  • Carpooling, lunch, gear, etc.

Bring

  • Use the gear checklist & cookies!

Sample Email to WKC Trip Leader

Hi,

My name is Shred Betty and I am a current member of the WKC and would like to sign up for your “name of river” trip on the XX date. I took the WKC intro to kayaking class in the spring of 2022 and have been on # of rivers including the _____ section of the _______ river. I am working on my roll in the pool and am successful about 50% of the time. I love wave trains, but still have trouble reading water and would be interested in following a more experienced boater down the river. I paddle a Dagger Mamba.

I have an awesome roof rack that can hold 4 boats including my own and don’t mind running shuttle. (Or—I don’t have a car that is really good for a shuttle vehicle/ I am planning on carpooling with Commando Kayaker)

A few questions for you:

  • Would this be a good run for someone with my skill level?
  • Can I walk any of the hard rapids if needed?
  • Is it possible to follow someone down the river since I have never run it before?
  • Is space available? Where and when you are planning to meet?
