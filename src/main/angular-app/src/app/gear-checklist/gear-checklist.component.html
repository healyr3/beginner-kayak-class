Pool Gear Checklist

Required

  • Whitewater Hard-Shell Kayak
  • Float Bags for back of kayak (front also, if long)
  • Whitewater Helmet
  • Whitewater Paddle
  • Whitewater Sprayskirt (needs to fit your kayak)
  • Whitewater PFD (personal floatation device)
  • Pool Wear (could include: swimsuit, shorts, wicking top, neoprene, rash guard)
  • Towel (used on river to change under)
  • Complete set of dry clothes (after on-water activity)

Optional

  • Nose Plugs (can go upside down w/o getting water up your nose?)
  • Earplugs
  • Goggles
  • Sponge
  • Extra pads, foam, duck tape, etc. for boat outfitting
  • Booties
  • Paddling Gloves or Pogies

River Gear Checklist

Required

  • Whitewater Hard-Shell Kayak
  • Float Bags for back of kayak (front also, if long)
  • Whitewater Helmet
  • Whitewater Paddle
  • Whitewater Sprayskirt (needs to fit your kayak)
  • Whitewater PFD (personal floatation device)
  • Drysuit (recommended) or Drytop/Wetsuit combo
  • Booties
  • Paddling Gloves or Pogies
  • Helmet Liner/Hat (for warmth)
  • Socks (wool/synthetic)
  • Wicking Base Layer (capilene, polopropylene, wool, fleece)
  • Wicking Insulation Layer (capilene, polypropylene, wool, fleece)
  • Towel (used on river to change under)
  • Complete set of dry clothes (after on-water activity)
  • Water or Hydration drink (bottle attached to inside of boat)
  • Dry bag/box (to keep lunch dry & needs to attach to inside of boat)
  • Lunch (energy bars, sandwich, trail mix, etc.)
  • Discover Pass ($10 per day, $30 annual pass)

Recommended

  • Nose Plugs (can go upside down w/o getting water up your nose?)
  • Whistle
  • Sunscreen
  • Wallet (wear and stow on self - not in boat)
  • Extra vehicle keys (possibly hidden on car)
  • Sponge

Optional

  • Sealskinz, Goretex Socks
  • Earplugs
  • Visor for helmet
  • Sunglasses w/ croakies
  • Zipper Wax
  • Paddle Wax
  • SealSaver
  • Extra pads, foam, duck tape, etc. for boat outfitting
